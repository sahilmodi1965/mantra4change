# Programs

Named initiatives Mantra4Change runs or convenes. Built up across a 12-year history (2013–present); the about-us page narrates the journey in nature metaphors (Seed → Sapling → Budding → Branching → Flowering).

## Flagship

### STEP — School Transformation and Empowerment Program
Launched 2013 in 7 pilot schools in Bengaluru. Scaled across Bihar, Punjab, and Uttar Pradesh by 2019–20. Now multi-state with customised variants.
Source: [mantra4change.org/about-us](https://www.mantra4change.org/about-us/), [mantra4change.org/steps](https://www.mantra4change.org/steps/)

### Shikshagraha
"A people's education movement dedicated to improving 1 million public schools in India by 2030." Mantra4Change presents itself as creator/driver. (ShikshaLokam co-organises InvokED and frames itself as catalyst — see `state/ecosystem.md` for cross-brain note. Per INTENT principle 7, this brain uses Mantra4Change's framing of Shikshagraha.)
Source: [mantra4change.org/shikshagraha](https://www.mantra4change.org/shikshagraha/)
