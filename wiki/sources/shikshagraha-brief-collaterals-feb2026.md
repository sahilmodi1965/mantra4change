## Brochure / 2-pager structure (the canonical content outline)

The doc lays out the canonical 7-section structure for any Shikshagraha pitch collateral:

- **Open with question:** "How do we work together to achieve education equity in our lifetime?" *or* "What is the education leadership needed to an effective public education system in India?"
- **A. Challenge / Problem** — zoomed-out stats (scale, dropouts, learning gaps, negative perception) + zoomed-in framing (top-down tunnel vision, unempowered leadership, fragmented efforts)
- **B. Vision** — verbatim: *"Every child of today's India, completes their education- Grade 1-12, in an environment that values and enhances their potential—through a community-led, locally empowered, and continuously evolving public school system."*
- **C. Roadmap for Exponential Change** — Local Problems Local Solutions / Leaders at Every Level / Leveraging Collective Strength
- **D. Implementation** — three collectives with stats above
- **E. Community** — logos of 9 strategic + 21 momentum partners
- **F. CTA** — "Join the movement, get in touch khushboo@shikshalokam.org"
- **G. Social handles** — Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, website, QR
