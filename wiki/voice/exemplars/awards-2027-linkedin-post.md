## Mantra4Change amplification voice

When Mantra4Change amplifies an Awards announcement on its own channel (e.g. Pratik posts on M4C LinkedIn or in an M4C newsletter), the draft must respect:

1. **Institutional credit first** — SFPI is named in full at first mention; Mantra4Change never claims Awards ownership.
2. **Movement framing, not partner framing** — the Awards belong to Shikshagraha, hosted by SFPI. Mantra4Change leaders (Khushboo, Santosh) are publicly associated; Mantra4Change is one of many partners.
3. **The three-line audience frame** is canonical — `Women leading communities. Youth igniting change. Teachers and school leaders building the future from within the system.` — use verbatim.
4. **Multilingual CTA** if the M4C amplification is for a national audience; English-only fine for English-only audiences.
5. **Mantra4Change's own voice rules still apply** — simple grounded language, active voice, no jargon, hopeful but honest.
