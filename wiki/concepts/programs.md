# Programs & mechanisms

How the movement becomes visible on the ground, and the five thematic areas it works through.

## How the movement becomes visible

- **Shiksha Chaupals** — community dialogue forums, led by women, held in villages across India
- **District improvement efforts** with governments and civil society organisations
- **Leadership development** for school heads, teachers, youth, and communities
- **Storytelling** that builds pride in public education
- **Shikshagraha Awards** — celebrating public education leadership
- **Shiksha Samvaad** — national convenings that build shared understanding
- **Digital public goods** that help leaders document and share progress

**The district is the unit of change.** See [[mission]] § What we do.

## The five thematic collectives

The areas through which Shikshagraha works toward future readiness. Use these when writing for themed grants, sector-specific awards, or thematic campaigns:

1. **Foundational Learning** — every child reading and doing maths by Grade 3
2. **STEM, Scientific Temper & Responsible AI** — curiosity, reasoning, and digital capability
3. **Environmental Consciousness** — children who understand and act on climate realities
4. **Career Readiness & 21st Century Skills** — creativity, collaboration, critical thinking, problem-solving
5. **Inclusion** — girls, marginalised communities, and children with disabilities at the centre, not the margin

## Four pillars of action

Shikshagraha works with **Samaj (society), Sarkar (government), Bazar (private sector), and Sanchar (media)** — together. Not one of them alone. See [[ecosystem]] for partner mapping under each pillar.

## Mantra4Change program portfolio (operational / legacy)

These are the operational programs Mantra4Change runs that contribute to the movement. Use this section when content is about Mantra4Change-as-org (MoUs, grants, hiring, partner pitches) rather than Shikshagraha-as-movement.

### STEP — School Transformation and Empowerment Program
Launched 2013 in 7 pilot schools in Bengaluru. Scaled across Bihar, Punjab, and Uttar Pradesh by 2019–20. Now multi-state with customised variants.
Source: [mantra4change.org/about-us](https://www.mantra4change.org/about-us/), [mantra4change.org/steps](https://www.mantra4change.org/steps/)
