## Mothers of Courage — the film

In Bihar, one of India's most under-resourced states, change rarely begins in classrooms. It begins in conversations.

*Mothers of Courage* is a film about women who transform traditional village gathering spaces into powerful platforms for educational change. Sitting together with youth, parents, educators, and government representatives, these mothers question long-held beliefs, confront systemic and cultural barriers, and redefine their shared aspirations — especially for their daughters.

**It is proof that the most profound change begins with the simple courage to speak.**
