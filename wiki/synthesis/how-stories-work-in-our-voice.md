## How stories work in our voice

Per [[voice]]:
- **Collective action, not individual heroes** — name communities and roles, not single-named protagonists as saviours
- **Human story first, data second** — start in the village or the conversation
- **Optimism is non-negotiable** — even the hardest stories close on possibility
