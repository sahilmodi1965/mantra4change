## What the brain knows about us

**Mission** *(from `wiki/concepts/mission.md`, user-validated by Pratik May 2026)*

> Shikshagraha is a people's movement to strengthen all 1 million public schools in India — so every child, regardless of where they are born, can learn well and be ready for the future. **We are not an NGO. Not a programme. A movement.** Mantra4Change is one of the partner organisations within this movement. Founded November 2023 by Khushboo Awasthi; that decade of Mantra4Change work (started 2013) became Shikshagraha.

**Voice — Always rules** *(from `wiki/voice/styleguide.md`, Pratik's AI Communication Brief)*

- Simple, grounded language — from the field, not a boardroom
- Hopeful but honest — name the problem without being defeated by it
- Active voice, short sentences
- Human story first, data second
- Collective action, not individual heroes
- Optimism is non-negotiable — even when we talk about what's hard

**Voice — Never rules**

- No jargon: never "leverage," "ecosystem," "scalable solutions," "holistic approach," "intervention"
- No passive voice
- No condescension toward government schools or communities
- No inflated or unverified impact claims
- No academic distance
